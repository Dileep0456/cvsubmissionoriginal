Instructions for CV Submission

  1. Tailor Your CV: Customize your CV to match the specific job you're applying for. Highlight relevant experiences, skills, and accomplishments that align with the job description.
  2. Follow Application Guidelines: Carefully read the submission guidelines provided by the employer. This includes file format (e.g., PDF), naming conventions, and any additional documents or information required.
  3. Proofread and Edit: Ensure your CV is free of typos, grammatical errors, and formatting issues. A well-polished CV reflects your attention to detail and professionalism.
  4. Include a Cover Letter: Attach a cover letter that complements your CV. Use the cover letter to introduce yourself, explain your interest in the position, and briefly highlight your most relevant qualifications.
